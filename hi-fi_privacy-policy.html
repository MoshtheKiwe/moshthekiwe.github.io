Privacy Policy

Hi-Fi Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Hi-Fi is designed as a local music player. The app lets you import audio files from your device, organize them into stations, and play them back on your device.

Hi-Fi does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit your personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Hi-Fi does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or contact information
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Your photos or videos
  • Your browsing history
  • Analytics or advertising identifiers
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to us
  • Listening history sent to us
  • Any data for advertising or tracking

Audio Files and Stations

When you import audio files into Hi-Fi, those files are stored locally on your device so the app can play them back and organize them into stations.

Imported audio files, station names, track names, playback state, and related library information remain on your device. They are not uploaded to any server by Hi-Fi.

If you delete stations or audio files from within the app, the app removes its local copy of that content from its own storage.

Default Sample Files

Hi-Fi may include a small default sample station with bundled audio files so users can try the app immediately. These bundled files are included with the app and do not involve collecting any user data.

Network Access

Hi-Fi does not require an account and does not send your music library or personal data to a remote server.

If a future version adds online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are released.

Third-Party Services

Hi-Fi does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, data brokers, or tracking SDKs.

App Store Privacy

Hi-Fi does not collect data as user data is processed and stored locally on the device and is not transmitted off-device to the developer or third parties.

Children's Privacy

Hi-Fi does not collect personal information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. The updated version will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
