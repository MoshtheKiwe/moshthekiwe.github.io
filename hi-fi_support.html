Need help with Hi-Fi?

Hi-Fi is a local music player that turns your audio folders into radio-style stations. If something is not working as expected, use this page to find quick help or contact support.

Local Audio Files

Hi-Fi plays music from files you import into the app. Your imported music is stored locally on your device and organized into stations.

If a file does not play, try checking that the file is a supported audio format and that it can play correctly in another audio app.

Lock Screen Playback

Hi-Fi supports standard iPhone media controls. While music is playing, you can use the Lock Screen to pause, resume, skip, or go back to the previous track.

Privacy

Hi-Fi is designed for local playback. It does not require an account, and your imported music stays on your device.

For more details, see the Hi-Fi Privacy Policy.
